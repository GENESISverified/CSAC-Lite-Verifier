# Governance

CSAC-Lite Verifier is designed to support organizations that need clearer governance around important digital decisions.

## Governance value

- Repeatable review process
- Clear status communication
- Better documentation discipline
- Easier internal reporting
- Improved accountability for decision workflows

## Typical governance users

- Public-sector reviewers
- Compliance officers
- Audit teams
- Risk teams
- Institutional operators

## Principle

A critical digital result should be explainable, reviewable, and reportable.
