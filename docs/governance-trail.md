# Governance Trail

The governance trail is the record left after review.

It helps organizations understand what was reviewed, what status was assigned and what decision context was preserved.

## What it preserves

- submitted outcome;
- review status;
- decision context;
- human review notes;
- escalation status;
- reporting summary.

## Why it matters

Without a governance trail, important outcomes can become difficult to explain later.

The trail supports audit, reporting, accountability and institutional memory.

## Principle

A critical outcome should leave a usable record after review.
