# Use Cases

CSAC-Lite Verifier supports organizations that need clearer trust signals around important digital outcomes.

## Public sector

Review eligibility, ranking, arbitration, or grant-related outputs with a consistent verification status.

## Financial services

Support review workflows for scoring, risk outputs, and compliance-sensitive decisions.

## Compliance teams

Create a repeatable way to present verification outcomes during internal or external review.

## Audit teams

Help reviewers move from scattered evidence to a clear status summary.

## AI governance

Support review of model-assisted outputs where traceability and clarity matter.

## SaaS operators

Add a verification layer to workflows that produce high-value or regulated results.
