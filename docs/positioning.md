# Positioning

Genesis Verified positions CSAC-Lite Verifier as a decision assurance surface for critical digital outcomes.

## Focus

The focus is not generic reasoning analysis.

The focus is the institutional review of an outcome that must be understood, recorded, and reported.

## What CSAC-Lite Verifier supports

- Review of submitted verification packages
- Clear status communication
- Audit-ready summaries
- Governance-oriented records
- Evaluation before wider adoption

## Why this matters

In high-value workflows, the question is not only whether a system produced a result.

The question is whether reviewers can understand the status of that result and keep a usable decision record.

## Principle

A critical outcome should not remain a black-box statement. It should become a reviewable object.
