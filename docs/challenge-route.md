# Challenge Route

A challenge route gives reviewers a clear way to question a status before it becomes trusted.

## Purpose

The route prevents important outcomes from being accepted only because they appear complete or automated.

## When to challenge

A challenge may be needed when:

- context is incomplete;
- the status is unclear;
- the outcome carries material consequence;
- a reviewer sees weak signals;
- escalation is required.

## Review value

A challenge route does not block responsible action.

It creates a disciplined pause before trust becomes operational.

## Principle

A critical outcome should remain challengeable before it becomes institutional truth.
