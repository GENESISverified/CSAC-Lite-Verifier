# Sectors

Genesis Verified is designed for environments where digital outcomes can carry material consequence.

## AI systems

Outputs that influence scoring, ranking, filtering, recommendation or judgement.

## Public sector

Eligibility, grants, procurement, arbitration and citizen-facing workflows.

## Critical infrastructure

Operational reviews where context, status and records must remain explainable.

## Finance and compliance

Risk outputs, compliance status, audit summaries and reporting workflows.

## Smart cities

Urban data, sensors, mobility, energy and multi-stakeholder public decisions.

## Technology translation

Evidence and review status when innovation moves toward deployment.
