# Contact

CSAC-Lite Verifier is maintained under the Genesis Verified initiative.

## Evaluation requests

Organizations interested in evaluation can prepare:

- target use case;
- organization type;
- expected review workflow;
- reporting needs;
- adoption timeline.

## Good fit

CSAC-Lite Verifier is best suited for teams that need clearer review of important digital outcomes.

## Principle

Start with a concrete decision workflow and define the status your reviewers need to understand.
