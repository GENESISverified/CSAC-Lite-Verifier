# Operating Model

Genesis Verified is built around clear review roles.

The operating model helps organizations avoid unclear responsibility around important digital outcomes.

## Roles

### Requester

Submits the outcome for review.

### Reviewer

Reads the status, context and available references.

### Service owner

Understands the operational consequence of the outcome.

### Governance owner

Keeps the review aligned with audit, compliance and reporting needs.

## Review discipline

Each role should know what it can decide, what it must escalate and what record it must preserve.

## Principle

A review process becomes stronger when responsibility is visible.
