# Rare Signal Review

Rare signals are low-frequency signs that may carry high review value.

They should not be ignored only because they appear uncommon.

## Purpose

Rare signal review helps teams notice unusual patterns, hesitation, mismatch or repeated exceptions.

## Examples

- uncommon status combination;
- unusual reviewer concern;
- repeated edge case;
- context that does not fit the normal pattern;
- decision consequence that is higher than expected.

## Principle

A rare signal can be more important than a frequent metric.
