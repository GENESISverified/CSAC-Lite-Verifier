# Platform

Genesis Verified is a critical outcome review platform.

It helps teams turn important digital outcomes into reviewable decision records.

## Platform model

The public platform is built around four questions:

- What outcome is being reviewed?
- What status has been assigned?
- What context makes the status meaningful?
- What record remains after review?

## Core layers

- Critical Outcome Review
- Decision Context
- Human Review Layer
- Governance Trail

## Public and controlled access

The public site explains the doctrine, vocabulary and evaluation path.

Controlled access is reserved for guided demonstrations, review templates and institutional evaluation.

## Principle

A critical outcome should not move directly from system output to institutional trust.
