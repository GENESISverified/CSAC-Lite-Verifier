# Review Workflow

The Genesis Verified workflow is intentionally simple.

It gives organizations a calm sequence for reviewing important digital outcomes.

## Sequence

### 1. Receive

Bring the submitted result into a controlled review surface.

### 2. Assess

Read the status, context and available review references.

### 3. Challenge

Pause automatic acceptance and allow responsible human review.

### 4. Record

Keep a usable trace for governance, audit and reporting.

## Principle

The workflow exists to slow down blind trust without blocking responsible action.
