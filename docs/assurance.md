# Assurance

CSAC-Lite Verifier supports clearer review, governance, and reporting around critical digital outcomes.

## Assurance goals

- Improve confidence in important results
- Make review workflows easier to understand
- Support audit-ready summaries
- Help teams document verification outcomes
- Reduce ambiguity around digital decisions

## Review principles

- Clear status
- Consistent summary
- Documented package information
- Operator-friendly presentation
- Responsible evaluation before production use

## Outcome

The service helps organizations present verification results in a way that reviewers can understand and use.
