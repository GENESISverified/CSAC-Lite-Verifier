# Status Lifecycle

A review status should be treated as a living review object, not a permanent truth.

## Lifecycle

### Draft

The outcome has been submitted but not fully reviewed.

### Reviewable

The outcome has enough structure to be examined.

### Challenged

A reviewer has identified a question, uncertainty or weak signal.

### Escalated

The outcome requires a higher level of review.

### Recorded

The status and review context have been preserved.

## Purpose

The lifecycle helps organizations avoid treating an early status as final.

## Principle

A status is useful only when its review stage is visible.
